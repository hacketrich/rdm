Just About RDM Charity Foundation

The charity Foundation is a branch of the Revival Discipleship Ministries which got its first existence on the 08th May 2021 after an extended discussion meeting attended by the members of the Ministry. The idea came into existence after an analysis of what can be the RDM so as to help the community in other areas of theirs needs additional to the preaching of the Gospel. Due to limited funding at the beginning, it marked a good idea of establishing projects that might bring a significant impact to the society for a long course of time as well in accordance the capacity. Additionally, as a part of Christianbased ministry, the main core of all activities to be performed is to demonstrate the love and impact of Jesus in everyone’s life in physical matters.

The Charity foundation is estimated to be unlimited in its lifetime as long as the father ministry will be running and for that, its aspects and activities are expected to be increasing, changing, all depending on what will be needed or more important and viewed to be more effective.
